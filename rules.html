Double Digit subtraction

Rules for the quiz
  • There will be 2 rounds and 5 questions in each round for each team upto the regional level
  • Time allotted : - 20 seconds for each question. 10 seconds for passed over question
  • Point awarded : 2 points for each question. 1 Bonus point for passed over question
  • A team may give a no. of responses in the allotted time of 20 seconds, however, each participant can answer only once. The correct answer shall be declared only after 20 seconds are over. Individual participants shall receive points too. This rule does not apply at the state level.
  • A total of 6 students –Best 6 (no reserve candidate)candidate should be selected upto the regional level.
  • The third round will be ‘Rapid Fire Round’ at the state level wherein 1 minute is allotted to each team in terms to answer 8 questions and 1 point is awarded to each correct answer.
  • At the state level a team will be selected as earlier (no change).
